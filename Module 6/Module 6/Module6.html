This website is about butterflies

A butterfly is an insect belonging to the order Lepidoptera, characterized by its colorful wings and a life cycle that includes four stages: egg, larva (caterpillar), pupa (chrysalis), and adult. Butterflies are known for their beauty and are often found in gardens, meadows, and forests. They play a crucial role in ecosystems as pollinators, helping to fertilize plants. Additionally, butterflies exhibit a wide range of colors and patterns, which can serve purposes such as camouflage, warning predators, or attracting mates.
